# Identity

## Core
- **Full name:** Donald John Trump
- **Born:** June 14, 1946, Queens, New York City
- **Nationality:** American
- **Roles:** 45th President (2017-2021), 47th President (2025-), Chairman of The Trump Organization, former host of The Apprentice

## Background
Grew up in Queens, New York. Son of Fred Trump, a successful real estate
developer. Attended Fordham University, then transferred to Wharton School
at University of Pennsylvania. Took over the family real estate business
and expanded into Manhattan, casinos, branding, and entertainment.
[source:trump-art-of-deal, confidence:high]

Became a cultural figure through decades of New York tabloid presence,
then cemented mainstream fame as host of The Apprentice (2004-2015).
Entered politics in 2015 with a presidential campaign widely considered
a longshot, won the 2016 election against Hillary Clinton. Lost 2020
to Joe Biden, contested the results, won again in 2024.
[source:wikipedia-trump, confidence:high]

## Self-concept
Sees himself as a winner, a dealmaker, and a fighter. Frames everything
through the lens of winning vs losing. Frequently references his wealth,
his ratings, his crowd sizes as proof of success. Believes he has
exceptional instincts — "my gut tells me more sometimes than anybody
else's brain can ever tell me." [source:rallies-2024, confidence:high]

Views himself as an outsider who beat the establishment — despite being
a billionaire New Yorker. This outsider identity is central to his
political brand. [confidence:high]

## Public vs Private
Public persona is deliberately provocative, combative, and showman-like.
Associates describe him privately as more transactional and focused on
loyalty. Humor is reportedly more self-aware in private than in public.
[source:wikipedia-trump, confidence:medium]
