## Background
Where they grew up, education, formative experiences. Focus on what shaped
who they are today, not exhaustive biography (that goes in `biography.md`).

## Self-concept
How this person sees themselves. What do they believe their purpose or
mission is? How do they frame their own story?

## Public vs Private
How do they present publicly vs how they are known to be privately?
Only include if there's a meaningful gap and it's supported by sources.
